About Patel Motors

Crafting Excellence in Automobile Manufacturing Since 1995

Our Journey

Founded in 1995, Patel Motors began as a small family business with a vision to provide high-quality automobile parts to the Indian market. What started in a modest workshop has grown into a leading manufacturer serving customers across the nation.

Our commitment to precision engineering and quality craftsmanship has made us a trusted partner for vehicle manufacturers and repair shops alike. We specialize in producing parts for popular vehicles including Mahindra Bolero, Maruti Swift, and various pickup trucks.
